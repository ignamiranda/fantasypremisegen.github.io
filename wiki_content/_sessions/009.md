# {{page.title}}

**{{page.in-game-date}}**

The gang drives an hour east to drop off Golftor at Gob Hill, a small gnomish settlement tucked away behind some hills. Gobara, the town leader, meets them as they drive in. Golftor tells the gang to find Professor Hardon, who will have a way of procuring more silver weapons.

Friday picks up pink rocks, meets Goblemon

Chef spends rent at Gobathecary

Friday is recommended to meet Gobeddy Bear and trades his rocks

They all ask Golftor and Gobara for help tracking down the nymphs and cry

They find Sosileis and track down the nymphs new encampment

They scare Bartender and accomplice and rescue the Jimmys from the bonfire

Sosileis tries to stop them at Bartender’s request, who is riding an elk. They escape.

On the road they make a deal to make the Jimmys some robotic legs to become taller.

They stop at Scary’s Lodge and meet Steve Steve, Sally Steve, and Andy Darkshadow. Chef tells Scary that he’s a wolf while ordering his food and Andy attacks. They beat up Moonshadow and Andy makes a run to escape.
